Dermatology

Dermatology is the medical specialty focused on the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail disorders. Dermatologists address a wide range of conditions, from common issues like acne, eczema, and psoriasis to more serious diseases such as skin cancer.

Oncology

Oncology is the branch of medicine that specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of cancer. Oncologists are trained to understand the complexities of various cancer types, including their development, progression, and response to treatment.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics is the branch of medicine dedicated to the health and medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. Pediatricians specialize in diagnosing and treating a wide range of childhood illnesses, from common conditions like colds and allergies to more serious diseases and developmental issues.

Gynecology

Gynecology is the branch of medicine that specializes in the health care of women, particularly concerning the female reproductive system. Gynecologists are trained to diagnose and treat a variety of conditions, including menstrual disorders, hormonal imbalances, and reproductive health issues.
